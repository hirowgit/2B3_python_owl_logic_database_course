Draft Genome Sequence of Enteropathogenic Escherichia coli, Isolated from the
Bloody Stool Sample of a Common Marmoset (Callithrix jacchus).

Hayashimoto N(1), Morita H(2), Inoue T(3), Yasuda M(4), Yamamoto M(2), Itoh T(3).

Here, we report the draft genome sequence of Escherichia coli strain R811. This
bacterium was isolated from the bloody stool sample of a common marmoset, and was
categorized as enteropathogenic E. coli because it possessed eae.

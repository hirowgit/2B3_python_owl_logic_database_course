Comparison of different methods and cell lines for isolating measles virus.

Forthal DN(1), Blanding J, Aarnaes S, Peterson EM, de la Maza LM, Tilles JG.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Medicine, University of California, Irvine College of Medicine, 
Orange 92668.

Infectivity titers were determined for seven strains of measles virus by using
various methods and cell lines. The use of B95-8 cells in a shell vial assay
resulted in the highest infectivity titers. Our data suggest that B95-8 is the
cell line of choice for the isolation of measles virus.

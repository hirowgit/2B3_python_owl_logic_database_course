Arthritis of the spondyloarthropathy variety in Callithrix jacchus.

Rothschild BM(1).

Author information: 
(1)Arthritis Center of Northeast Ohio, Youngstown 44512.

Sixty skeletons of Callithrix jacchus were examined to identify
spondyloarthropathy. Erosive disease in Callithrix was confirmed as
spondyloarthropathy on the basis of diagnostic sacroiliac erosions, syndesmophyte
formation, and the nature and distribution of the peripheral joint erosions.

Environmental enrichment for laboratory marmosets.

Byron JK(1), Bodri MS.

Author information: 
(1)Delaware Valley College, Small Animal Science and Conservation, Delaware
Valley College, Doylestown, PA 18907, USA.

The authors report on using two environmental enrichment devices for marmosets,
and suggest the design of five other devices that may be more successful in
stimulating foraging or grooming behavior than the devices tested.

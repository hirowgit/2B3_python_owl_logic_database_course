Hyperinfection by Strongyloides in captive callitrichids with progressive weight 
loss syndrome.

Conga DF(1)(2), de Souza NF(3), Jaques AMCC(2), Benigno RNM(1), Pereira
WLA(1)(2).

Author information: 
(1)Programa de Pós-Graduação em Saúde e Produção Animal na Amazônia, Universidade
Federal Rural da Amazônia (UFRA), Belém-Pará, Brazil.
(2)Laboratório de Patologia Animal, Instituto da Saúde e Produção Animal (ISPA),

(3)Programa de Pós-graduação em Medicina Veterinária, Faculdade de Medicina

We report three cases of disseminated hyperinfection by Strongyloides in primates
Callithrix jacchus and Callithrix penicillata in captivity and with progressive
weight loss syndrome. Histopathological examination revealed severe lesions in
lungs, liver, and intestines with the presence of Strongyloides larvae and in a
generalized manner, including other organs such as the pancreas and heart.

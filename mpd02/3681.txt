An illustrated guide to endotracheal intubation in small non-human primates.

Morris TH(1), Jackson RK, Acker WR, Spencer CK, Drag MD.

Author information: 
(1)Department of Laboratory Animal Science, SmithKline Beecham Pharmaceuticals,
Harlow, Essex, UK.

Endotracheal intubation allows precise delivery of inhaled anaesthetic agents.
Intubation in small non-human primates (less than 1 kg), is straightforward,
using commercially available equipment, and careful positioning of the animal.
Equipment and methods are fully described and illustrated.

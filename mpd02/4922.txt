Comments on the taxonomy of Brazilian marmosets (Callithrix, Callitrichidae).

Hershkovitz P.

The taxonomic appraisal of Brazilian marmosets, genus Callithrix, by
Coimbra-Filho and Mittermeier is critically examined. It is shown that their
assertions are not supported by the evidence.

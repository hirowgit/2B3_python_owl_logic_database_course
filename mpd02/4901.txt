Anti-brucella agglutinins in bats and "Callithrix" monkeys.

Ricciardi ID, Nunes MP, Andrade CM, Da Silva AG.

Anti-Brucella agglutinins were found in 5 of 53 (9.4%) vampire bats Desmodus
rotundus, captured in the State of Bahia, Brasil. Two specimens of Diphylla
ecaudata were negative. Fifty specimens of the small monkey, Callithrix
penicillata, were also negative.

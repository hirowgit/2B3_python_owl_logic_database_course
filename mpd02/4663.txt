Serum biochemical, hematological and body measurement data for common marmosets
(Callithrix jacchus jacchus).

Yarbrough LW, Tollett JL, Montrey RD, Beattie RJ.

Blood samples were drawn and body measurements taken from healthy adult and
juvenile common marmosets which had been housed under controlled environmental
conditions for a minimum of 2 years. Blood was analyzed for serum biochemical and
hematological constituents.

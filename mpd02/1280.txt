Comment on
    Curr Biol. 2015 Feb 16;25(4):424-34.

More vision is preserved after removal of primary visual cortex in infant than
adult primates. A recent study suggests that this is due to the preservation of a
retina-to-pulvinar-to-cortex pathway that normally regresses during development.

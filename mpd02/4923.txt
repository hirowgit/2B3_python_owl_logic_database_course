Clinical use of CT1341 anaesthetic ("Saffan") in marmosets (Callithrix jacchus).

Phillips IR, Grist SM.

An intramuscular dose of 18 mg/kg bodyweight of this steroid anaesthetic produced
surgical anaesthesia with rapid induction, safe, efficient maintenance and rapid 
recovery without side effects.

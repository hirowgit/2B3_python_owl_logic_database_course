A case report of spontaneous opening of congenitally fused labia in a female
common marmoset (Callithrix jacchus) followed by pregnancy and birth of twins.

Wedi E(1), Nayudu PL, Michelmann HW.

Author information: 
(1)Laboratory of Reproductive Biomedicine, German Primate Center, Goettingen,
Germany. edris1@web.de

A first case of spontaneous opening of congenitally fused labia (CFL phenotype)
in a captive common marmoset followed by pregnancy and birth is presented here.
The occurrence of this phenotype has been previously published in captive
marmosets, but so far the etiology is unknown.

Influence of season on birth distribution in marmosets and tamarins.

Brand HM.

The cotton-topped tamarin in captivity exhibits a seasonal influence on birth
distribution, while the captive silvery marmoset, common marmoset, Goeldi's
monkey and red-mantled tamarin are not significantly affected.

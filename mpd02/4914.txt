Management of marmosets for biomedical research.

Hearn JP, Lunn SF, Burden FJ, Pilcher MM.

The management of a successful breeding colony of common marmosets, Callithrix
jacchus, is described, including details of treatment during the conditioning
period after arrival from the wild. Methods are described for the collection of
blood, urine, and vaginal washings. Suggestions are made for improving the
conditions of importation of these animals from South America.

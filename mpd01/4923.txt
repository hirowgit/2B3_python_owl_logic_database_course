Clinical use of CT1341 anaesthetic ("Saffan") in marmosets (Callithrix jacchus).

Phillips IR, Grist SM.

An intramuscular dose of 18 mg/kg bodyweight of this steroid anaesthetic produced
surgical anaesthesia with rapid induction, safe, efficient maintenance and rapid 
recovery without side effects.

4924. Lab Anim. 1975 Jan;9(1):49-56.

Diagnosis of pregnancy in marmosets (Callithrix jacchus).

Mitchell SJ, Jones SM.

3 techniques for studying reproductive function in the marmoset are described.
Abdominal palpation was found to be the most successful method for diagnosis of
pregnancy. Vaginal cytology and urinary immunoassays met with limited success and
were of supportive value only.
